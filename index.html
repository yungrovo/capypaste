About me, Robin

That's me at the left, the human being who made this website.

About capybaras

There are so many reasons why the capybara is my favorite animal, so here are a few facts for you. I hope I can be a capybara in my next life.

Capybaras are often seen hanging out with other animal species and generally relaxing hardcore.

They are the largest rodent in the world. Despite their extremely chill personality, the only South American country they are not naturally found in is Chile. Guess the joke doesn't translate...

Capybaras enjoy sitting around in mud! They can sleep underwater with just their noses sticking out.
